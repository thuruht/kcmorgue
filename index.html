According to factual data from policescorecard.org, KCMO PD:

Kills more people than 95% of US police departments. Kills more unarmed people than 91% of departments. Has an about 77% white force that both arrests and kills more black and brown people than they do any other group.
KCPD also: solves substantially more homicides of white people than they solve homicides of any other group, uses more "less-lethal" force than 71% of police departments, has more funding than 96% of departments, and spends over three million dollars a year settling lawsuits.
